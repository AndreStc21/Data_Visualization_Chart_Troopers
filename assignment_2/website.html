CO2 Emissions Flow Analysis

Comparing the emission flows of countries and their respective regions

Top 3 per region - 2022 CO2 emissions flow

Richer and more populous countries tend to have higher emissions, both from fossil fuels and land use.
As expected, China, India, and the USA are the largest polluters, followed by Russia, Indonesia, Brazil, and Germany.
Interestingly, while land-use emissions are typically higher in wealthier nations, they are also significant in Brazil and the Democratic Republic of Congo, likely due to deforestation in areas like the Amazon rainforest.
